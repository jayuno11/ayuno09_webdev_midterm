Tourist Spots in Taiwan

Taipei 101

A noteworthy skyscraper with 106 floors.

Lungshan Temple

Histroic Buddhist and Taoist temple.

Jioufen

Jiufen is a mountain town in northeastern Taiwan, east of Taipei. It's known for the narrow alleyways of its old town, packed with teahouses, street-food shacks and souvenir shops.

Shi-men Ting

A city district known for fashion stores.

National Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall

It is a famous national monument, landmark and tourist attraction erected in memory of Generalissimo Chiang Kai-shek, former President of the Republic of China.

Shi Lin Night Market

It is a night market in Shilin District, Taipei, Taiwan, and is often considered to be the largest and most famous night market in Taiwan.
